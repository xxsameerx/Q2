Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower located on the Champ de Mars in Paris. It is named after the engineer Gustave Eiffel, whose company designed and built the tower. Constructed in 1889, it is a global cultural icon of France and one of the most recognizable structures in the world.

Address:

Champ de Mars, 5 Avenue Anatole France, 75007 Paris, France
